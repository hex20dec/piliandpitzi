Pili and Pitzi Say Thank You

Talia V.
February 19, 2023
Updated on February 19, 2023

Pili and Pitzi were playing in the jungle when they saw their friend, a parrot, flying toward them with something in her beak.

"Hi, Pili and Pitzi!" the parrot chirped. "I have a gift for you from the other side of the jungle!"

The parrot dropped a small package in front of Pili and Pitzi and flew away. The package was wrapped in bright green paper and had a red ribbon tied around it.

Pili and Pitzi were thrilled to receive a gift, but they weren't quite sure what to do next. They looked at each other, and Pili said, "We should open it!"

As they unwrapped the gift, they found a set of toy cars inside. They played with the cars for a while and had a great time. But as they finished playing, Pitzi looked at Pili and said, "We should say thank you to our friend for the gift."

Pili nodded in agreement, and they both knew that the right thing to do was to write a thank you note to their friend.

The next day, they found a piece of paper and some pencils and wrote a thank you note to the parrot. They thanked her for the gift and for being such a wonderful friend. They then rolled up the note and flew to the other side of the jungle to deliver it to their friend in person.

When they arrived at the parrot's tree, they found her playing with some other animals. Pili and Pitzi climbed up to her and handed her the note.

The parrot opened the note and read it aloud. She was so happy and touched by their gratitude that she started to cry.

"You're so welcome, Pili and Pitzi," the parrot said. "I'm so glad you liked the gift. And thank you for writing me such a sweet note."

From then on, Pili and Pitzi made sure to always write a thank you note or make a call to thank someone for a gift. They learned that showing gratitude and appreciation is important in maintaining strong and healthy relationships with others.

The end.
