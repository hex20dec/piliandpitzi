Excuse Me, Pili and Pitzi!

Talia V.
February 19, 2023
Updated on February 19, 2023

Pili and Pitzi were on a picnic in the park. They had just finished a delicious meal of bananas and peanuts when Pili let out a loud burp. Pitzi was surprised and a bit embarrassed by the sound.

"Pili, that was really loud and a little bit rude," said Pitzi.

Pili was taken aback. "I'm sorry, I didn't mean to be rude," he said. "I just had a lot of peanuts."

"It's okay, Pili," said Pitzi. "But next time, try to remember to cover your mouth when you burp. And if it happens again, just say 'Excuse me'."

Pili nodded and promised to be more careful. He didn't want to be rude or make Pitzi feel uncomfortable.

A few minutes later, Pitzi let out a big sneeze. "Achoo!" she exclaimed.

Pili quickly remembered the lesson Pitzi had just taught him. "Bless you, Pitzi," he said. "And don't worry, you don't need to say 'excuse me' when you sneeze. It's just a natural reflex."

Pitzi smiled and thanked Pili for his kind words. They finished their picnic and headed back home, making sure to be mindful of their manners along the way.
