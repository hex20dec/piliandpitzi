Pili and Pitzi's Jungle Adventure

Talia V.
February 19, 2023
Updated on February 19, 2023

One sunny day, Pili and Pitzi were exploring the lush jungle when they stumbled upon a group of animals they hadn't met before. The animals were gathered around a tree, chatting and laughing.

"Hello there, I'm Pili and this is Pitzi" said Pili, introducing herself and Pitzi. "We're new to the jungle. What are you all up to?"

"We're having a fruit party," said a friendly monkey, extending his paw. "My name is Momo. It's nice to meet you!"

Pili and Pitzi shook Momo's paw and replied, "It's nice to meet you too, Momo!"

As they chatted and got to know each other, Pili and Pitzi felt happy to have made some new friends in the jungle.

Soon, a chatty parrot named Polly joined the group. "Hello everyone! I'm Polly. Nice to meet you, Pili and Pitzi!" she said, flapping her colorful wings.

"Nice to meet you too, Polly!" said Pili and Pitzi, shaking Polly's wing.

Pili turned to the group. "It was great meeting you all, and we're so glad we could join your party," she said. "Hey Momo, do you want to play some games with us?"

Momo and the other animals perked up at the suggestion, and soon they were all playing games and having fun together.

After a while, it was time for Pili and Pitzi to head back home. "Thank you for the fruit party, Momo and Polly," said Pili. "It was a pleasure meeting you and your friends."

Momo and Polly smiled and replied, "Likewise, Pili and Pitzi. We hope to see you again soon!"

As Pili and Pitzi walked away, they talked about the importance of using proper manners, "Remember, it's important to introduce yourself politely when you meet someone new, and to use proper etiquette by shaking their hand and saying 'it's nice to meet you.' And don't forget to say 'it was a pleasure meeting you' when you leave."

"Absolutely," agreed Pitzi. "Using proper manners helps us make new friends and show respect to others."

Pili and Pitzi smiled as they walked through the forest, grateful for the kind and friendly animals they had met that day.

The end.
