Pili and Pitzi's Manners Lesson: Always Flush the Toilet

Talia V.
February 19, 2023
Updated on February 19, 2023

One sunny day in the jungle, Pili the elephant and Pitzi the mouse were playing outside when they needed to use the bathroom. They walked to the public restroom and saw that someone had left the toilet unflushed and the seat up. Pili and Pitzi looked at each other and frowned.

"That's not very polite," said Pili.

"Yeah, it's not fair for the next person who needs to use it," added Pitzi.

They both knew that they should always flush the toilet and put the seat down. It was just good manners.

As they were washing their hands, Pili and Pitzi noticed a sign on the wall that said "Please flush the toilet and put the seat down."

"Look Pitzi, the sign says the same thing we were just talking about," said Pili.

"Yeah, it's important to be respectful of others who need to use the bathroom too," said Pitzi.

From that day on, Pili and Pitzi always made sure to flush the toilet and put the seat down whenever they used the bathroom, whether at home or in public places. They also made sure to remind others to do the same.

By being considerate of others and following good bathroom etiquette, Pili and Pitzi showed that they were not only polite, but also respectful and kind. And that's what good manners are all about!

The end.
