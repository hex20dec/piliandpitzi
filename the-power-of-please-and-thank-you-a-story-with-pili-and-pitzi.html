Pili and Pitzi

The Power of Please and Thank You: A Story with Pili and Pitzi

Talia V.
February 18, 2023
Updated on February 18, 2023

Once upon a time, in a lush green forest, there lived an elephant named Pili and a tiny mouse named Pitzi. They were the best of friends and loved to play together.

One sunny day, Pili and Pitzi decided to take a stroll in the forest. As they walked along, they saw a group of animals having a picnic. The animals had brought along delicious treats like sandwiches, fruits, and cookies.

Pili and Pitzi's mouths watered at the sight of the food. "Can we have some food, please?" asked Pili, but the animals didn't seem to hear her. Pitzi tugged at Pili's trunk and said, "We need to say 'please' to get their attention."

Pili looked at Pitzi and smiled. "You're right, Pitzi. Let's try again." Pili raised her trunk and said, "Excuse me, can we please have some food?"

The animals looked at Pili and Pitzi and smiled. They were happy to share their food with their friends. Pili and Pitzi thanked the animals and enjoyed the delicious treats.

After they finished eating, Pili and Pitzi continued their walk in the forest. They came across a stream where a turtle was trying to cross. "Can you help me cross the stream?" asked the turtle.

"Of course!" said Pili, and she reached out her trunk to help the turtle. After they helped the turtle cross the stream, Pitzi reminded Pili, "Don't forget to say thank you to the turtle for letting us help."

Pili turned to the turtle and said, "Thank you for letting us help you cross the stream." The turtle smiled and said, "You're welcome."

As Pili and Pitzi continued on their walk, they realized how important it was to use "please" and "thank you" when talking to others. They knew it made others feel appreciated and respected. From that day on, Pili and Pitzi always remembered to use these words and encouraged their friends to do the same.

The end.
